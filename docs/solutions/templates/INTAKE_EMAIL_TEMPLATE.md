## Instructions

Send this email to the client contact after the Readiness Assessment scores GREEN or YELLOW with remediation complete. Customize the bracketed fields before sending.

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** ClubOS Intake - Materials Needed by [DATE]

---

Hello [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for completing the Readiness Assessment. We are ready to begin intake for your ClubOS implementation.

**What We Need From You**

Please provide the following materials by **[DEADLINE DATE]**:

1. **Member Data Export** - Current member roster with required fields
2. **Event History Export** - Past 12 months of events (if available)
3. **Policy Documents** - Membership tiers, event rules, officer roles
4. **Completed Intake Workbook** - Attached to this email

**File Naming Convention**

Please name all files using this format:

```
[ORGNAME]_[DOCTYPE]_[YYYYMMDD].[ext]

Examples:
SBNC_MemberExport_20251215.csv
SBNC_EventHistory_20251215.csv
SBNC_IntakeWorkbook_20251215.md
SBNC_MembershipPolicy_20251215.pdf
```

**Where to Upload**

Upload all materials to: [SECURE UPLOAD LINK]

Do not send files via email attachment. Use the secure upload link only.

**What We Will Do**

- Validate data exports against schema requirements
- Map your policies to ClubOS configuration
- Identify gaps or conflicts requiring your decision
- Prepare a Migration Blueprint for your review

**What We Will NOT Do**

- Write or rewrite your policies
- Make governance decisions on your behalf
- Interpret ambiguous rules without your explicit input
- Proceed if required materials are missing

**Reference Documents**

For detailed requirements, please review:

- INTAKE_SCHEMA_GUIDE.md - Field requirements and formats
- INTAKE_DELIVERABLE_BUNDLE.md - What you will receive from us

These documents are attached or available at [DOCUMENTATION LINK].

**Questions?**

Reply to this email with any questions. We will respond within [RESPONSE SLA].

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
ClubOS Implementation Team
